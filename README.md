
# Hi, I'm Sayali 👋

I'm a Computer Science postgraduate with a foundation spanning **QA/Testing Engineering**, **Java
development**, and growing hands-on experience in **Data Science & AI**.

At Worldline Global Services, one of Europe's largest payment processors, I worked as a Testing
Engineer on a live credit card payment system — resolving GUI and database-layer defects, running
regression and process testing, and communicating findings clearly to both technical and
non-technical teams.

Alongside that, I build independent projects to develop my data science and AI skills, including a
deep-learning medical image classifier and Tableau dashboards for business analysis.
